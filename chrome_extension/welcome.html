Thanks for installing Stream Monitor!

You're all set. The extension will automatically close Twitch tabs when a streamer you're watching raids someone else.

How it works

  • Connects to the Stream Monitor desktop app on your PC
  • Tracks tabs opened for your monitored streamers
  • Detects raids and automatically closes the tab
  • Optional auto-mute for opened streams

Get started

Make sure the Stream Monitor desktop app is running in your system tray. Click the extension icon in your toolbar to see the debug panel and toggle settings.
